About CELLINFO

Your one-click destination for mobile phone information and comparison.

Who We Are

CELLINFO.com is an initiative by ASKME Ltd., a multinational company providing information services to the general public. ASKME has been serving customers by delivering reliable product information through various channels.

With the increasing demand for mobile phone information, CELLINFO was created to provide users with quick and accurate mobile phone details through an online platform.

The Challenge

ASKME Ltd. identified that a large number of customer queries were related to mobile phones. These included questions about features, prices, and comparisons between different models.

  • High volume of mobile-related queries
  • Time-consuming explanations for call executives
  • Increased operational cost
  • Difficulty in explaining detailed features over calls

Our Solution

Centralized Information

All mobile phone details are available on a single platform for easy access.

Brand-Wise Browsing

Users can explore mobile phones based on their preferred brands.

Easy Comparison

Side-by-side comparison helps users make informed buying decisions.

Why Choose CELLINFO

  • Saves time for users
  • Reduces call center workload
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Accurate and updated mobile information
  • Downloadable detailed specifications

Our Vision

To become a trusted digital platform for providing reliable and comprehensive mobile phone information.

Our Mission

To help users make informed decisions by offering accurate, simple, and easily accessible mobile phone details.
